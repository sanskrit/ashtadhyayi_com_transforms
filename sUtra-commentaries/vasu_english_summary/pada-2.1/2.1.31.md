---
index: 2.1.31
sutra: पूर्वसदृशसमोनार्थकलहनिपुणमिश्रश्लक्ष्णैः

---
A word ending in Instrumental -3rd case-affix is compounded with the words पूर्व 'prior', सदृश 'like', सम 'similar', अनर्थ 'words having the sense of less', कलह 'quarrel', निपुण 'proficient', मिश्र 'mixed' and श्लक्षण 'polished sleek', and the resulting compound is तत्-पुरुष ।
