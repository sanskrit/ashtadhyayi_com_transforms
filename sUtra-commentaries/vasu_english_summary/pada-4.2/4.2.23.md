---
index: 4.2.23
sutra: विभाषा फाल्गुनीश्रवणाकार्त्तिकीचैत्रीभ्यः

---
The affix ढक् optionally comes after the words 1. फाल्गुनी 2. कार्त्तिकी and 3. चैत्री being the names of full-moon nights, the whole word so formed being a Name denoting a division of time (a month, a fortnight or a year) in which the moon is full.
