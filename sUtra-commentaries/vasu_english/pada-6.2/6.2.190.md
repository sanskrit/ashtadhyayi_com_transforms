---
index: 6.2.190
sutra: पुरुषश्चान्वादिष्टः

---
After अनु the acute accent (उदात्त) falls on the final of पुरुष when it means a man of whom mention was already made.

The word अन्वादिष्ट means 'of a secondary importance, inferior', or 'mentioned again after having already been mentioned'. Thus अन्वादिष्टः पुरुषः = अनुपुरुषः꣡; but अनुगतः पुरुषः = अनुपुरुषः ॥
