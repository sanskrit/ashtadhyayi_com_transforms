---
index: 3.1.55
sutra: पुषादिद्युताद्यॢदितः परस्मैपदेषु

---
The verbs classed as पुषादि, are a sub-division of the 4th conjugational verbs; and द्युतादि verbs belong to the Ist conjugation. As पुष्, अपुषत् 'he nourished'; द्युत्, अद्युतत् 'he shone'; गम्लृ, अगमत् 'he went'. But not so in the <i>Atmanepada</i>; as व्यद्योतिष्ट, अलोतिष्ट.
