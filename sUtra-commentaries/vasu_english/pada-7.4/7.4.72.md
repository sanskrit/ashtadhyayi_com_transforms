---
index: 7.4.72
sutra: अश्नोतेश्च

---
After the lengthened आ of the reduplicate of अश् (अश्नोति) comes the augment नुट् (न्) before the short अ in the लिट् (Perfect Tense).

This applies to a case where the root does not contain a double consonant. Thus व्यानशे, व्यानशाते, व्यानशिरे ॥ The rule applies to अश् (<i>Svadi</i> 18) and not to अश् (<i>Kryadi</i> 51); there we have आश, आशतुः, आशुः ॥
