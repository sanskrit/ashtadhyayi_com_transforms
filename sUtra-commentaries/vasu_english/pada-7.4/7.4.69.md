---
index: 7.4.69
sutra: दीर्घ इणः किति

---
As ईयतुः, ईयुः ॥ These are thus formed इ + अतुस् = य् + अतुः (य् substituted by इ [[6.4.81]]). Then there is reduplication, the य् becomes <i>sthanivat</i> to इ by (I. I. 59), and we have इ य् + अतुः and then by the present rule इयतुः ॥ Why do we say before a कित् affix? Observe इयाय, इययिथ ॥
