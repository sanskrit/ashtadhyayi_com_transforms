---
index: 7.4.51
sutra: रि च

---
As कर्तारौ, कर्त्तारः, अध्येतारौ, अध्येतारः ॥ The स् of अस् 'to be' is elided in the Perfect, because it is there that a र् beginning affix can follow it. Thus व्यतिरे (See [[6.4.111]]. According to the reading of <i>Padamanjari</i>, there can be no example of अस् ॥ अस्तेरुदाहरणं न प्रदर्शितं, रेफादेरसम्भवात् ॥
