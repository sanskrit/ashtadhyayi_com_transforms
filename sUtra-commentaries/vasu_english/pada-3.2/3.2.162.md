---
index: 3.2.162
sutra: विदिभिदिच्छिदेः कुरच्

---
As विदुरः पण्डितः 'a knowing <i>Pandit</i>'; भिदुरं काष्टम् 'a splitting or fragile wood'; छिदुरा रज्जुः 'a cutting or breaking rope'. The affix is employed in a reflexive sense after the verbs भिद and छिद.



Vart:- The affix कुरच् comes after the verb व्यध 'to kill'; and there is vocalisation of the semivowel; as विधुरः ।
