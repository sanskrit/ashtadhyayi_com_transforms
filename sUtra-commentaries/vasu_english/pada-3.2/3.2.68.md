---
index: 3.2.68
sutra: अदोऽनन्ने

---
The <i>anuvritti</i> of the word '<i>Chhandasi</i>' does not extend to this <i>sutra</i>.



Thus आममत्ति =आमात् 'eating raw food'; शस्यात् 'granivorous'.



But with अन्न as an <i>upapada</i>, we have अन्नादः 'eating food', which is formed by the general affix अण् (III 2. 1).
