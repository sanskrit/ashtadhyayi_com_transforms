---
index: 3.2.161
sutra: भञ्जभासमिदो घुरच्

---
As भञ्ज् + घुरच् = भङ्ग् + डर [[7.3.52]] = भङ्गुरः 'brittle'; the palatal being changed into guttural, because of the indicatory घ. So also मेदुरः पशुः 'a fat beast'; भासुरं ज्योतिः 'splendid light'.



After the verb भञ्ज् this affix gives a reflexive signification-- that which naturally breaks of itself.
