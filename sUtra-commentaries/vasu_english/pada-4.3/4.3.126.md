---
index: 4.3.126
sutra: गोत्रचरणाद्वुञ्

---
This debas अण् as well छ ॥ Thus <i>gotra</i> :- ग्लौचुकायनकम्, औपगवकम् ॥ According to <i>Patanjali</i>, the word चरण which ordinarly means Vedic School, means here duties or doctrines (<i>dharma</i>), and traditions (<i>amnaya</i>). Thus कठानां धर्म आम्नायो वा = काठकम् 'the <i>dharma</i>-<i>Sutras</i> or the tradition belonging to the school of <i>Kathas</i>'.  So also कालापकम्, मौदकम्, पैप्पलादकम् ॥
