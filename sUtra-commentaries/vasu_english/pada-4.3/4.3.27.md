---
index: 4.3.27
sutra: संज्ञायां शरदो वुञ्

---
This debars अण् ॥ Thus शारदका दर्भाः 'the grass called <i>Saradaka</i>'. शारदका मुद्गाः 'the pulse called <i>Saradaka</i>.'



Why do we say संज्ञायां ? Observe शारदं सस्यम् 'the autumnal crop.'



According to some, the word संज्ञायां governs all the succeeding <i>sutras</i> up to [[4.3.38]].
