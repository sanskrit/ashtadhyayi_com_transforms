---
index: 4.3.95
sutra: भक्तिः

---
The word भक्ति in the <i>sutra</i> means 'serving', 'worshipping' or 'loving'.



Thus स्रुघ्नो भक्तिरस्य = स्रौघ्नः 'a person who is loyal to <i>Srughna</i>'. So also माथुरः, राष्ट्रियः &c.



The <i>anuvritti</i> of the word अभिजनः ceases. The word सः and अस्य should be read into the <i>sutra</i>.
