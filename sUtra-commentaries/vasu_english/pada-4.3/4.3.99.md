---
index: 4.3.99
sutra: गोत्रक्षत्रियाख्येभ्यो बहुलं वुञ्

---
This debars अण् and छ. Thus: ग्लौचुकायनक 'who loves or serves &c <i>Gluchukayani</i>.' So also औपगवकः, कापटवकः ॥ <i>Kshatriyas</i>:- नाकुलकः, साहदेवकः, साम्बकः ॥



The word आख्यां in the <i>sutra</i> indicates that the words should be the names of well-known or famous <i>Kshatriyas</i>, and not of any body who is a <i>Kshatriya</i> by profession.



The word बहुल shows the non-universality of this rule. Thus पाणिनोर्भक्तिरस्य = पाणिनीयः, पौरवीयः ॥



The word गोत्र in does not mean here the Grammatical <i>Gotra</i>, but a word formed by a patronymic affix in general.
