---
index: 2.1.31
sutra: पूर्वसदृशसमोनार्थकलहनिपुणमिश्रश्लक्ष्णैः

---
From this <i>sutra</i>, we learn incidentally that the words पूर्व, &c., govern the instrumental case.



As, मासेन पूर्वः = मासपूर्वः 'prior by a month,' संवत्सरपूर्वः 'prior by a year,' मातृसदृशः 'like the mother,' पितृसदृशः 'like the father,' मातृसमः 'similar to the mother,' पितृसमः 'similar to the father,' माषोनम्, माषविकलं 'less by a <i>masha</i>, असिकलहः'a fight with the swords,' वाक्कलहः 'bandying words.'वाग् निपुणः 'proficient in speech,' आचारनिपुणः 'proficient in the observances of sacred duties,' तिलमिश्रः — गुडमिश्रः 'mixed with sesamum or jaggery' आचारश्लक्षणः 'polished in behaviour.' 



Vart :-The word अवर should be enumerated in the list, as मासावरः 'later than a month.'
