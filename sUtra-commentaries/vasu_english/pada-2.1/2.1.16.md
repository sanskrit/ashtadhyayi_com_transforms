---
index: 2.1.16
sutra: यस्य चायामः

---
As, अनुगङ्गं वाराणसी 'the city of <i>Benares</i> extends alongside the river Ganges' अनुयमुनं मथुरा  <i>Mathura</i>, alongside of the <i>Yamuna</i>, the length of <i>Mathura</i> being measured by that of the <i>Yamuna</i>.'



Why do we say 'length'? Observe वक्षमनु विद्योतते विद्युत्.
