---
index: 4.1.34
sutra: विभाषा सपूर्वस्य

---
The word सपूर्वस्य means when the word पति is not an <i>upasarjana</i>, but stands at the end of a compound. Thus वृद्धपतिः or वृद्धपत्नी; स्थूलपतिः or स्थूलपत्नी ॥ This is an example of <i>aprapta</i>-<i>vibhasha</i>. Because it is free from the limitations of '<i>yajna</i>-<i>sanyoga</i>' of the last <i>sutra</i>. Why do we say when it is preceded by another word? Observe पतिरियं ब्राह्मणी ग्रामस्य, this <i>Brahman</i> lady is the mistress of the village.' Here the feminine is पति and not पत्नी ॥
