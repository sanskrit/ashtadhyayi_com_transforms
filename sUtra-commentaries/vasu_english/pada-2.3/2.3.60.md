index: 2.3.60
sutra: द्वितीया ब्राह्मणे

---
Ex. गामस्य तदहः सभायां दीव्येयुः (<i>Maitr</i>.S.1.6.11) In the Vedic literature the simple verb <i>div</i> takes the accusative instead of the genitive. And with <i>upasargas</i> it is optional. The <i>anuvritti</i> of the word शेष does not extend to this <i>sutra</i>, or the following.
