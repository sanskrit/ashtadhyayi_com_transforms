---
index: 7.2.50
sutra: क्लिशः क्त्वानिष्ठयोः

---
As क्लिष्ठ्वा or क्लिशित्वा, क्लिष्टः or क्लिशितः, क्लिष्टवान् or क्लिषितवान् ॥ क्लिशू (IX. 50) having an indicatory ऊ would have optionally taken इट् before <i>ktva</i> by [[7.2.44]], but then the <i>Nishtha</i> affixes by [[7.2.15]] would never have taken the augment. क्लिश् (<i>Divadi</i> 52) उपतापे, being <i>anudatta</i> would always have taken इट् before <i>ktva</i> and <i>Nishtha</i>. Hence this rule ordains option with regard to <i>ktva</i>.
