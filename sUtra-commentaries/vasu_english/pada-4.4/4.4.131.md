---
index: 4.4.131
sutra: वेशोयशआदेर्भगाद्यल्

---
The ल् of यल् shows that the accent falls on the vowel preceding the affix (VI. I. 193). Thus वेशोभगो विद्यते यस्य स = देशोभ꣡ग्यः 'strong-fortune' so also यशोभ꣡ग्यः 'famous-fortune'. The word वेश means strength': भग means 'fortune, desire, effort, greatness, virility and fame.' The word वेशोभगः may be taken as a <i>Dvandva</i> compound of वेशस् 'force' and भगः 'fortune.' The word वेशोभग्यः would then mean 'possessed of power and fortune.'
