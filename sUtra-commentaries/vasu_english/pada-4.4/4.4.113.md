---
index: 4.4.113
sutra: स्रोतसो विभाषा ड्यड्ड्यौ

---
This debars यत् which comes in the alternative. As स्रोतसि भवः = स्रोत्यः॑ or स्रोत्यः॒॒꣡ the difference being in the accent [[3.1.3]] and [[6.1.185]]. The <i>anubandha</i> ड causes the elision of अस् of स्रोतस् ॥ When यत् is added the form is स्रोतस्यः॑ ॥
