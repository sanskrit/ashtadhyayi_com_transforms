---
index: 4.4.95
sutra: हृदयस्य प्रियः

---
The construction is shown by the aphorism itself. Thus हृदयस्य प्रियः = हृ꣡द्यः 'pleasant to the heart' e. g. हृद्यो देशः, हृद्यं वनम् ॥ But we cannot say हृद्यः पुत्रः, the word संज्ञायां [[4.4.99]] governs this <i>sutra</i> also, and thus restricts the meaning of the word.
