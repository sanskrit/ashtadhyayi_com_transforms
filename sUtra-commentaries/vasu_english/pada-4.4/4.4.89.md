---
index: 4.4.89
sutra: संज्ञायां धेनुष्या

---
This word is formed by adding षुक् and य to धेनु ॥ It has <i>udatta</i> on the final. धेनुष्या means that cow (धेनु) which is given to a creditor in discharge of a debt or as a pledge to satisfy the debt from her milk. It is also called पीतदुग्धा ॥ As धेनुष्यां भवते ददामि ॥
