---
index: 8.3.63
sutra: प्राक्सितादड्व्यवायेऽपि

---
The root सित् occurs in <i>sutra</i> [[8.3.70]]. Thus [[8.3.65]] teaches ष change: as अभिषुणोति, परिषुणोति, विषुणोति, निषुणोति ॥ So also when अट् intervenes; as अभ्यषुणोत्, पर्यषुणोत्, व्यषुणोत्, न्यषुणोत् ॥ The force of अपि is that the change takes place even when the augment अट् does not come, i. e. in cases other than the augment.
