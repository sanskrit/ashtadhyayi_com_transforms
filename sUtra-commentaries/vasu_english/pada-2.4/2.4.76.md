---
index: 2.4.76
sutra: बहुलं छन्दसि

---
Elision does not take place where ordained, and takes place where not ordained. दाति प्रियाणि (instead of ददाति) 'he gives pleasant objects,' धाति instead of दधाति ।



So also there is <i>slu</i>-elision of the <i>vikaranas</i> after roots other than those belonging to the <i>Juhotyadi</i> class. Thus; पूर्णां विवष्टि, जनिम विवक्ति.
