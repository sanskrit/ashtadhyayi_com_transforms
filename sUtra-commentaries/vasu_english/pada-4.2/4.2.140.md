---
index: 4.2.140
sutra: राज्ञः क च

---
The letter क is the substitute of the final of the word राजन् , when छ is added.

This <i>sutra</i> only teaches substitution: for राजन् would have taken छ by [[4.2.114]]. Thus राजकीयम् ॥ The word देशे does not govern this <i>sutra</i>, not being appropriate.
