---
index: 6.1.120
sutra: अनुदात्ते च कुधपरे

---
Thus अयं सो अग्निः (<i>Yaj</i>. 12. 47), अयं सो अध्वरः ॥ Why do we say when अ is gravely accented ? Observe अधोऽग्रे, here अग्रे has acute accent on the first syllable. Why do we say "when followed by a Guttural (कु) or a ध"? Observe सोऽयमग्निः सहस्त्रियः ॥
