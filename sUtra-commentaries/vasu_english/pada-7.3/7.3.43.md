---
index: 7.3.43
sutra: रुहः पोऽन्यतरस्याम्

---
As व्रीहीन् रोपयति or रोहयति ॥ According to <i>Padamanjari</i> this <i>sutra</i> could be dispensed with. The form रोपयति could be obtained from the root रुप् of <i>Divadi</i> class : which though meaning मोहन may be taken to mean 'grow' also; अनेकार्थत्वाद् धातूनाम् ॥
