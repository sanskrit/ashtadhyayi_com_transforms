---
index: 3.4.58
sutra: नाम्न्यादिशिग्रहोः

---
The affix णमुल् comes after the verbs 1. आदिश् 2. ग्रह् 'to seize' when the word नामन् 'name' in the Accusative -2nd case is in composition.

Thus नामादेशमाचष्ठे 'he mentions it, telling his name'. नामग्राहं मामाहूयति 'he calls me by taking my name (i.e. by my name)'.
