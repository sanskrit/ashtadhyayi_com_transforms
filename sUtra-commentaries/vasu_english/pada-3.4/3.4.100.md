sutra: इतश्च

---
Thus, for ति we have त्, for सि we have स्, &c, as in the above table. The इ of <i>Atmanepada</i> is, however, not elided, because the <i>anuvritti</i> of <i>Parasmaipada is understood here from </i>sutra_ [[3.4.97]]. Thus अपचत्, अपाक्षीत्, but अपचावहि and अपचामहि।
