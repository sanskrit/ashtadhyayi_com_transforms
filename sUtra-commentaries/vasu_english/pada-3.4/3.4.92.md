---
index: 3.4.92
sutra: आडुत्तमस्य पिच्च

---
In the लोट् (Imperative Mood) आट् is the augment of the affixes the first person and the termination is as if it had an indicatory प्।

This is clear. Thus in the <i>Parasmaipada</i>, we have आनि, आव, आम । Thus करवाणि, करवाव, करवाम; करवै, करवावहै, करवामहै ।
