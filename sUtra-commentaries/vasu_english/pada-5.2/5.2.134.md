---
index: 5.2.134
sutra: वर्णाद्ब्रह्मचारिणि

---
Thus वर्णिन् 'a <i>Brahmachari</i>'. The first three वर्ण or castes, who are entitled to the investiture with sacred thread, and lead the life of a <i>Brahma</i> student are so called. When the meaning is not of <i>Brahmachari</i> we have वर्णवान् ॥
