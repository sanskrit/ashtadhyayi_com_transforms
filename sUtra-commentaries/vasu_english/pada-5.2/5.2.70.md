---
index: 5.2.70
sutra: तन्त्रादचिरापहृते

---
The word तन्त्र must be in the fifth case in construction, by the very fact of its being so employed in the <i>sutra</i>. The word अचिर means 'short time, not long' अपहृत means "taken off". Thus तन्त्रादचिरापहृतः = तन्त्रकः 'a cloth just taken off the loom' i. e. an unbleached cloth. As तन्त्रकः पटः, तन्त्रकः प्रावारः 'a new mantle' = प्रत्ययः or नवः ॥
