---
index: 8.2.88
sutra: ये यज्ञकर्मणि

---
Thus ये३यजामहे ॥ Why do we say when employed in sacrificial work? Observe ये यजामह इति पञ्चाक्षरम् "<i>Ye</i> <i>yajamahe</i> consists of five syllables". Here it is simple recitation.



<i>Ishti</i>:- The word ये is <i>pluta</i> before यजामहे only. Therefore not so here: ये देवा॑सो दि॒॒व्येकाद॑शस्थ (<i>Rig</i>. I. 139. 11).
